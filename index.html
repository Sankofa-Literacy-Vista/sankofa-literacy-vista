Our Proprietary Method

DISCOVERY™
Framework for Literacy, Leadership & Learning

Every session at Sankofa Literacy Vista is built around our proprietary DISCOVERY™ Framework for Literacy, Leadership & Learning — a research-informed, nine-stage journey that builds real skills, develops confident communicators, and grows future leaders. No other program in Las Vegas teaches this way.

D
Decode Unlock Understanding

Build phonics, vocabulary, and reading comprehension from the inside out.

I
Investigate Ask Questions. Seek Truth.

Scholars explore topics deeply, ask thoughtful questions, and gather knowledge.

S
Synthesize Make Connections

Organize ideas and connect new learning to prior knowledge for deeper meaning.

C
Create Imagine. Plan. Produce.

Write, design, and produce original work using the RACER structured method.

O
Observe Look Closely. Think Deeply.

Sharpen attention to detail and develop critical thinking across every subject.

V
Voice Speak Up. Share Ideas.

Communicate thoughts confidently through speaking, writing, and listening.

E
Execute Take Action. Make It Happen.

Apply learning with focus and goals, and follow through with discipline.

R
Reflect Learn. Grow. Improve.

Think about progress, celebrate growth, and build a continuous improvement mindset.

Y
You Your Journey. Your Future.

Discover strengths, nurture passions, and develop leadership skills for life.

  • A named, trademarked instructional framework — no other center in Las Vegas has this
  • Every lesson purposefully maps to a stage of your scholar's growth
